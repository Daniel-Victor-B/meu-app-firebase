# **App Name**: MEI Flow

## Core Features:

- Monthly Financial Distribution Simulation: Allow users to input their monthly income and operational costs to simulate and visualize the distribution across taxes, pro-labore, and reserves.
- Annual MEI Limit Tracking: Provide a visual tool for users to monitor their accumulated revenue against the annual MEI limit, including projections and alerts for exceeding thresholds.
- Recommended Account Structure Guidance: Present a clear, card-based guide detailing a recommended 4-account system to separate business and personal finances effectively.
- MEI Operational Checklist & Guide: Offer comprehensive, step-by-step checklists for opening an MEI, performing monthly and annual tasks, and highlighting important regulatory considerations.
- Personalized Financial Advisory Tool: An AI tool that provides tailored financial advice and optimization suggestions based on the user's entered income, costs, and current MEI limit status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (positive indicators, key actions): A vibrant, professional green, symbolizing growth and clarity. HSL(160, 80%, 45%) / #2EDA8E.
- Background color: A deep, near-black dark gray with a subtle green tint, maintaining a clean and professional appearance. HSL(160, 10%, 7%) / #101312.
- Accent color (secondary highlights, call-to-actions): A brighter, slightly yellowish-green, providing clear contrast to the primary while staying within an analogous palette. HSL(130, 70%, 55%) / #68E65E.
- Main typeface for headlines and body text: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif), offering a modern, neutral, and highly readable foundation for financial information.
- Monospaced typeface for numerical data and code-like displays: 'Source Code Pro' (monospace sans-serif), ensuring clarity and distinction for specific financial values.
- Implement a clean, card-based layout with generous spacing, clear visual hierarchy, and intuitive navigation tabs for distinct content sections.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and micro-interactions, especially when switching tabs, updating financial calculations, or highlighting alerts, to enhance user experience without distraction.
